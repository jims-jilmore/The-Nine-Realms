Yggdrasil & the 9 Realms

In Norse Mythology the universe was made up of Nine Realms.

The 9 realms were connected by a massive sacred tree known as Yggdrasil.

The Nine Realms were known as:

  • Asgard
  • Midgard
  • Alfheim
  • Muspellheim
  • Vanaheim
  • Jotunheim
  • Niflheim
  • Helheim
  • Nidavellir
